Activists

The Washington Post is a morning daily newspaper published in Washington, D.C. It is the most widely circulated newspaper in the United States capital and is widely regarded as one of the best in the country. Given this situation I am researching, this news source tends to be very left-leaning as they are in favor of supporting those who are suffering from a social problem opposed to supporting the means that perpetuated this suffering.

Problem (Grounds)

Growth and development, according to economic theory, would virtually eliminate child labor. However, after some success in increasing the number of children in school and rehabilitating former child workers, the national effort to end the practice is losing steam, and child labor has increased significantly in major cities.

Why Should You Care? (Warrants)

You should care because the situation at hand is very much under the table. There is a large unregulated workforce that is highly vulnerable to exploitation and does not benefit from labor laws. Even where children are treated violently, legal restrictions have little impact in the informal economy, and a culture of impunity makes prosecution difficult.
